Medicare for All?
The Policy

Medicare-for-All is a proposed single-payer healthcare system co-sponsored currently by 118 federal representatives. It would eliminate the private health insurance industry and replace it with a comprehensive healthcare plan that would cover every American equally.

This plan would move the burden of healthcare expenses onto taxes, which would be based on a progressive tax system. This means that the wealthiest Americans would pay the most taxes on it. A single-payer system like this would make medicine, hospital visits, surgeries, etc. all free.
